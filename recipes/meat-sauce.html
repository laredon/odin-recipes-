Meat Sauce!

Sauce full of flavor!

I tweaked this recipe while locked down during covid with my girlfriend. We used to put it on veggies instead of pasta so we didn't get too many carbs. But this sauce goes well on everything, and it's delicious on it's own!

Ingredients!

  • pound of bacon
  • knob of butter
  • shallots
  • garlic
  • carrots
  • cayenne, parsley, oragano, dried basil, thyme
  • half cup of red wine
  • pound of ground beef
  • one cup of san marzano tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps!

  1. render the bacon down in a large sauce pot
  2. remove bacon and some grease, add the knob of butter, shallots, garlic, and carrots
  3. once carmelized deglaze with a hit of red wine
  4. add the ground beef and spices and cook till it's brown
  5. add the flour and stir for three minutes
  6. deglaze again with the rest of the red wine
  7. add the san marzano tomatoes
  8. bring to a boil, turn down heat, simmer for two hours
  9. taste for salt ad pepper, adjust as needed
  10. serve hot or save it later for reheating!
