Simple Greek Vinaigrette

This recipe is so easy and will only take 5 minutes or less to make

Ingredients!

  • red wine vinegar
  • olive oil
  • minced garlic
  • dried oregano
  • dried thyme
  • lemon zest
  • lemon juice

Steps

  1. put 1/4 cup of red wine vinegar into container
  2. add minced garlic and dried spices
  3. zest one lemon into container
  4. squeeze the juice from zested lemon into container
  5. put a lid on container and shake violently
  6. put this simple Greek Vinaigrette on any salad!
