Garlic Chicken

This recipe is kind of reckless, uses too much butter, and way too much garlic but you'll love it. PROMISE

Ingredients

  • two chicken breasts
  • about 20+ cloves of garlic
  • stick of butter
  • lemon juice for deglazing

Steps

  1. preheat your oven to 400 degrees
  2. get your cast iron skillet roaring hot
  3. sear chicken breast on one side in skillet
  4. once seared, throw in the whole stick of butter
  5. toss in all the garlic cloves, I'm talking enough garlic to make a vampire ill
  6. pop the skillet in the oven for about 15 minutes
  7. take skillet out of oven, squeeze two lemons into skillet
  8. put skillet back in oven for remaining 2-3 minutes, or until chicken reaches an internal temp of 152 degrees
  9. let chicken rest on counter for 5-8 minutes for carry-over cooking, allowing the chicken to reach 165 degrees
  10. enjoy!
